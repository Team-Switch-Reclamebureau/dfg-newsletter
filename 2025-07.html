Wellbeing of our People: Boost Yourself
To contribute to the pillar ‘Wellbeing of our People,’ work has been underway since the beginning of this year to develop the ‘Boost Yourself’ program. With Boost Yourself, we aim to get our employees moving – both literally and figuratively. The program consists of four components:
  • BOOST Samen (Together) – Strengthen your team spirit and enjoyment at work with fun and engaging activities.
  • BOOST Next – Develop yourself as an employee and discover new talents for the future with workshops and assessments.
  • BOOST Focus – Find mental calm and take control of your energy with training sessions, scans, and even break reminders.
  • BOOST Fit – Prevent or resolve physical discomfort with smart tools and targeted workplace advice.
Please not that this is not a mandatory program or imposed plan, but rather a set of personal choices. Please take some time to look at our ‘Boost Yourself’ website and learn more about all the different boosts.
