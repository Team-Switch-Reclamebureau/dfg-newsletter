Dear colleague,

Over the past year, we have been working on renewing our Sustainability strategy. With valuable input from all our DFG companies, we have co-created our Sustainability strategy towards 2030: IMPACT2030. In the past few weeks, Marcel en I shared the strategy with DFG companies and celebrated the launch by distributing the materials.

From now on, the strategy is externally available, and in this newsletter, you will find all the resources available to support you. Share these with the relevant colleagues within your organisation.

We look forward to working together with you on IMPACT2030 in the coming years!
Colourful regards,

Raimon Loman
Group Sustainability Manager
Dutch Flower Group
