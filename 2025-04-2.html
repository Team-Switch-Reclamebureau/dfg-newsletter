DFG is founding father of FSI, and is therefore committed to its target of sourcing certified product. In the attachment you can find the results for 2024, including a detailed analysis and forecast for 2025 and beyond.

Working together towards 100% certified sourcing
In recent years, DFG has voluntarily worked towards the goal of reaching 90% certified sourcing. As of 2025 we are taking it to the next level by working together towards the mandatory target of 100% certified sourcing by July 2027.
