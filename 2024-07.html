What is the most important focus point in e-Flora's sustainability strategy? Famke Brokking, Sustainability Manager at e-Flora, shares all the details. She also reveals which project excites her the most.
FAIRTRADE JAARCIJFERS

The total Fairtrade Premium generated from Fairtrade flower sourcing by DFG during 2023 was €3.1 million which was an increase of 1% compared to 2022. DFG sourced over 364 million Fairtrade flower stems. Since 2018 DFG flower sourcing has generated over €16 million in Fairtrade Premium for flower producers and their communities.
DUTCH FLOWER GROUP ENTERS INTO PARTNERSHIP WITH GREENHOUSE SUSTAINABILITY

Dutch Flower Group (DFG) will be using the Flori Footprint Tool developed by Greenhouse Sustainability to gain a reliable overview of the environmental impact of its products. All DFG Sustainability ambassadors attended a training about the Flori Footprint Tool.

BEE PARK AALSMEER COMPLETED: SEEDED WITH WILD FLOWERS

After planting 1,000 m² of perennials in March, 3,500 m² was seeded with wildflowers at the Bee Park VBA South in early June. With this, the 4,500 m² bee park is completed and will provide food for wild bees, other insects, and birds.
